Places

We recommend some of the best places in Sydney for you to admire its beauty and grandeur. Let yourself be seduced by the buildings and monuments of the big city that will leave you with your mouth open.

Opera House

One of the most well-known and impressive buildings worldwide. An icon of the city and Australia. A masterpiece of architecture. But do not stay alone with the outside, enter and enjoy its opera, ballet or theatre performances. You won't regret it.

Sydney Harbour

The world-famous port where the best known is located and what makes Sydney the city it is. Here are located the Sydney Opera House and the Sydney Harbour Bridge. A unique space where you can enjoy and admire icons of architecture.

Port Bridge

The bridge that gives access to Sydney Harbour is a monument in itself and is part of the iconic view of the city. Observe its shape and figure and decide to climb it if you dare.

Port Darling

Recreational port. Within walking distance of the city centre, you can enjoy many attractions, including the Sydney Aquarium and Sydney Zoo, as well as the Australian National Maritime Museum.

Port Jackson

The original port of Sydney. It is the site of the first settlement of Europeans in Australia. Port full of history and events, such as the celebration of New Year's Eve in Sydney.

The Rocks

The area is very close to Sydney Harbour. For this reason, it is one of the favourite places for tourists, for its activities, restaurants and magnificent views of the port. Essential visit.

Nature

Oasis in Sydney? Of course, enjoy Australian nature in every corner of the city and the surroundings. Natural paradises in their purest state for your enjoyment.

Royal Botanic Garden

In one of the corners of Sydney Harbour, you will find this paradise full of plants and animal species such as birds, bats and water dragons. Let yourself be captivated by the richness of Australian flora and fauna.

Blue Mountains

Just two hours by train from Sydney, you will find the Blue Mountains, with cliffs, waterfalls and valleys. Here, you can do activities such as mountain climbing, abseiling and guided tours. A wonderful landscape.

Whale watching

From May to November, you can observe these magnificent animals. Many cruise ships set sail from Sydney Harbour, offering you the chance to enjoy this natural experience.

Zoo Taronga

At Taronga Zoo you can observe wild animals, many of them threatened. It is a non-profit organization that deals with the protection of animals and the environment. It offers stunning views of Sydney Harbour.

Sydney Aquarium

Discover the largest collection of Australian marine life in the world and have fun with all the marine species found there. Ah! And dare to swim with sharks.

Beaches

What would Sydney be without its beaches, white sand and crystal-clear waters? Dive into the sea, play sports or just relax while experiencing first-hand why you'll love the city.

Bondi

The world-famous beach is located just 8 km from the city centre and offers endless possibilities. Surf, have fun on the sand, and visit cafes, bars, and markets... Bondi allows you to take a bath whenever you want throughout the year.

Manly

A 30-minute ferry ride from Sydney Harbour, Manly will get you to enjoy the sea with the activity of your choice. Surfing, swimming, shopping and even diving with sharks.

Palm Beach

In the north of Sydney, Palm Beach is the ideal choice. Fine sand and crystal-clear waters for surfing, swimming and relaxing. Also, enjoy the food with all the culinary options around.

Surf

Sydney's beaches are ideal for surfing. Watch surfers on the beaches or take action on your own. If you don't know, you can always find schools for beginners.

Navigation

Sydney is a beautiful city to discover by sea. You can ride on boats that tour Sydney Harbor and cruises that follow coastal routes and islands.

Luxury

The wealthiest have their place in the city with unforgettable first-class experiences that will make you enjoy them to the fullest. Just for you.

Luxury Hotels

The views of Sydney Harbour are incredible. Enjoy them with hotels like Four Seasons Hotel Sydney, Shangri-La Hotel Sydney, and InterContinental Sydney.

Sydney Besparke Tour

A luxury getaway to discover the best Sydney with several possible routes to suit your desires.

Hunter Valley

Visits for wine lovers, farm markets and quality restaurants in the countryside. Choose the stay you prefer and enjoy this spectacular valley.

Lord Howe Island

An island that is a paradise in itself. Nature, sea, luxury and relaxation.

Hydro Majestic

In the beautiful Blue Mountains, experience beauty with this hotel that runs along the rugged edges of these mountains.

Shopping

Shopping, shopping and more shopping. Surprise yourself with huge buildings dedicated to them and street stalls that will fulfil all your commercial desires

Queen Victoria Building

A historic and wonderful building full of fashion shops, jewellery, as well as bars and restaurants. It is the most famous shopping centre in Sydney.

Rocks Market

The market where you can find everything you are looking for is just a step away from Sydney Harbour.

Opal Collection

Australia's largest collection of opals in central Sydney. A place to visit if you are passionate about jewellery.

Sport

Why not a sport? Why not the ones you want whenever you want in Sydney? Practice your favourite and future sports in the city. Pass the ball!

Golf

You can play golf in numerous venues in Sydney, such as Riverside Oaks, an hour from Sydney, in resorts or the Hunter Valley. A sport within everyone's reach.

Surf

At Bondi Beach, you can learn Surf if you don't know yet. If you are an expert, take your board and go to the sea to enjoy.

Navegation

You can charter yachts with the captain just 20 minutes from central Sydney. You can also fish and kayak next to Sydney Harbour.

Parachuting

On the North Coast, you can jump out of a moving plane at a distance of more than 200 km/h for a good couple of seconds. The feeling of safe adrenaline.

Thunder Jet Boat

Board a motor boat on Sydney Harbour and enjoy the experience as the boat drifts through the water at full speed. A unique adventure.

Skiing

If you want to enjoy the snow, the Snowy Mountains are your best option. Also, you can always go skiing lessons if you don't know yet. You won't regret it, for sure.

Culture

Cultura, historia, conocimiento y mucho más. Si te interesa esto, Sydney es tu ciudad. Recorre las experiencias interactivas y didácticas que hay a tu disposición por todos los rincones.

Opera House

The Sydney Opera House is architecture and culture alike. Here you can find wonderful shows of numerous performing arts. Enjoy the culture in the best building.

Australian Museum

This museum has numerous collections dedicated to culture and natural science, such as dinosaurs and Australian wildlife.

National Maritime Museum

Discover Australia's naval history as you explore a submarine, a destroyer and a replica of an eighteenth-century ship.

PowerHouse Museum

The favourite place to discover science, technology, architecture, engineering and much more. Don't miss it.

Bradman Museum

If you like cricket, this is your museum. Learn all about the history and facts of this sport in Australia from the museum's technology.

Food

Hungry after the tour of Sydney? All planned. You have delicacies in the very centre of the city with unforgettable views. And food and wine tours to the outskirts.

Quay

It stands out for its magnificent location between Sydney Harbour and the Opera House and its food is fabulous. Enjoy the famous chocolate cake.

Opera Bar

Just a few steps from the Opera House, this bar serves delicious meals with an amazing view.

Sydney Harbour

In the magnificent harbour of Sydney, you will find many gastronomic offers that will suit your needs and budgets. Here you can find everything in one place.

Food and wine

Take food and wine tours at different locations within the Sydney area while enjoying nature.

Australia

Australia is a reason in itself to fall in love with Sydney. Country of wild nature, exclusive animals and different life. A huge, unique country with amazing people. Country of extremes, of unparalleled beauty and its mentality. Australia is a world apart and a new horizon to visit. Welcome to Australia.
